👉 ₹8.3 lakh per month
👉 ~₹27,500 per day (assuming 30 days)

If your average order value = ₹120, you need:
👉 ~230 orders per day

That’s achievable with:

1 busy location + evening rush
OR 2 strong time slots (lunch + dinner)
📍 2. Smart Location Strategy (THIS decides success)

Don’t move randomly—be predictable.

Ideal Daily Route:
Lunch (12–3 PM) → Offices / colleges / IT parks
Evening (5–10 PM) → Parks / markets / street food hubs
Weekly Anchors:
2–3 fixed high-footfall spots
2 event-based days (fairs, college fests, night markets)

💡 Key insight: Consistency builds repeat customers faster than roaming.

🍽️ 3. Menu Engineering (Profit-first approach)

You already built a great menu—now optimize for:

Keep ONLY:
12–15 fast-moving items
5 hero items (must sell 50% of volume)
Ideal Hero Items:
Paneer Roll
Chicken Roll
Masala Maggi
Fried Rice (veg/non-veg)
Lemon Rice / Poha (low cost, high margin)
Cost Rule:
Food cost per item ≤ 35%
Example:
Sell at ₹80 → Cost should be ₹25–30

💡 Simple menu = faster service = more orders = more revenue

💰 4. Revenue Boosters (Most people ignore these)
